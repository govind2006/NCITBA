ABOUT US

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering has been set up to impart training of the highest standards to the students, in the field of computers, thus preparing them to meet the exacting demands of the highly competitive global industrial market. The Department is currently focused on providing B.Tech., MTech., M.C.A. & PhD programs which cover exhaustively the area of Computer Science & Engineering.

OBJECTIVE

The International Conference on Information Technology and Data Analytics - 2019 (ICITDA-2019) aims to provide an outstanding opportunity for both academic and industrial communities alike to address new trends and challenges and emerging technologies on topics relevant to today’s fast-moving areas of Information Technology & Informatics. The vision of ICITDA-2019 is to develop faster communication among researchers and practitioners working in a wide variety of areas in Information Technology & Informatics with a common interest. In collaboration with Springer and partnering with Communications in Computer and Information Science (CCIS), this is the second conference in the series.
